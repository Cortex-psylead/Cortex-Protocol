# Code of Conduct — Cortex Protocol

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the standard

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Conduct that violates the sovereignty mandate of the protocol

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the Protocol Stewards. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.
